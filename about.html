🌟 Our Legacy

Founded in the year 2000, Gandhi Matriculation School has grown from humble beginnings into a proud institution of learning in Eriyur. Our founder and correspondent Mr. A. Kannan, with 33 years of teaching excellence, established this school with dedication and a vision to provide quality education to all. His remarkable service in the field of education earned him the prestigious Kalvi Semmal Award. Last year, we proudly celebrated our Silver Jubilee, marking 25 years of excellence.

🪷 Our Pillar of Strength

Mrs. Loganayagi Ma’am has been the unwavering pillar of Gandhi Matriculation School since its inception. Her commitment, encouragement, and constant support have played a crucial role in shaping the school’s success. She continues to inspire both students and teachers with her strength, wisdom, and care.
